Wack The Mole

Welcome to Wack The Mole

Rules:

  • 1. You have 5 lives and start with 0 points. If you miss a mole or fail to hit it in time, you lose a life.

  • 2. Each mole you hit gives you 100 points, and golden moles give you 500 points.

  • 3. At certain score thresholds, more holes open up, and the game speeds up. The changes are as follows:
    • From 0 to 2400 points: 4 holes.
    • From 2500 to 4900 points: 9 holes.
    • From 5000 to 7400 points: 12 holes.
    • At 7500+ points: 16 holes.

  • 4. You get to choose 3 different levels of the game: Baby-mode, normal-mode and pro-mode. Each mode has its own speed levels and end messages.

  • 5. The game ends when you reach 10,000 points or lose all your lives. If you lose, you retain the points you earned before losing all your lives.

    • Have Fun!
