The goal of my new portfolio in semester 4 (year 1) is to build it using Bootstrap.

It was made with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.

I originally wanted to use Tailwind for its flexibility and open-source nature, but my college told me to only use Bootstrap.


Here you can see my color and font schedule:

Color and font Decision

I chose the Gentle Made font for a cartoonish look to stand out to employers.

After asking for advice from friends, peers, teachers and family, I chose orange nav backgrounds with royal blue borders instead of gold with gray and royal blue borders.

The reason why I changed it is because the gold color caused me to have an headace as it is a very light color.

I also added a binary rain animation that spells “hello world” for a Matrix-style effect.
